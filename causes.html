Our Past Projects

NAMANJE PRIMARY BRICKS

Nipe Sauti Africa in partnership with the local community constructed bricks to build 5 classrooms for Namanje Primary School in Western Kenya. Initially, the kids were studying under a tree.

100 UNIFORMS CAMPAIGN

In late 2018 Nipe Sauti Africa intiated the 100 uniforms project to assist orphaned pupils in the native areas of Kenya. NSA rallied among members and friends and by February, 117 school uniforms were raised.

BUNGOMA ANTI-JIGGERS

In late 2016, Nipe Sauti Africa headed to Bungoma for the anti-jigger campaign where we raised awareness and sanitized the community to counter the huge jigger infestation that was initially there.

OLE NGURUONE ICT

In 2016, Nipe Sauti Africa partnered with Safaricom to supply 20 desktop computers to a Ole Nguruone Township Day Secondary School in Nakuru Kenya in a bid to improve computer literacy in the region.

About us

Our main aim is to put smiles on folks who would otherwise never be able to smile.
