ABOUT Nipe Sauti Africa

We are a charity organization that was founded at Strathmore University in 2015. The main motive for the formation of this organization was to support and give a voice to the voiceless in the society. At the time of our formation, we had about 5 members who were all students at Strathmore University.

We have since expanded and grown in numbers and have campus affiliations with Cooperative University, Multimedia University among others. We currently have more than 100 registered members who volunteer every week in our different activities.

Some of our regular activities include visiting the sick, prisions and children homes. We also do mentorships in schools as well as prisons. We have most recently started building study centers and hope to have one in every single county in the country.

Join us as we put a smile on people's faces!

our mission

Our mission is to actively be of service to others as an inspirational model of living and working together to create social and economic change in the Kenyan society and among ourselves.
