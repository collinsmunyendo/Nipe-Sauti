Our Chapters

MENTORSHIP

We dedicate our time mentoring some of the most vulnerable members of the community, including school going children, prisoners and the sick. Our goal is to positively contribute to bright futures for members of these communities.

ENVIRONMENT

We firmly believe that everyone has a role in preserving the environment and subsequently ensure our planet will be preserved for future generations. We plant trees as well as run campaigns aimed at creating environmental awareness.

HEALTH

At Nipe Sauti, we care about the health of all members in the society. We frequently visit the sick and have partnerships with organizations such as Texas Cancer Center where we usually volunteer in cleaning as well as donating blood.

COMMUNITY SERVICE

We pride ourselves in volunteering in initiatives that improve the livelihoods of community members. Some of these activities include visiting and donating to children homes, prisoners, hospital facilities as well as other community courses.

ENTREPRENUERSHIP

We believe in the power of entrepreneurship to empower communities. We therefore always come up with projects as well as supporting existing projects that can empower the most vulnerable members of the community.

PRO-LIFE

We firmly believe in advocating for good living standards across our members as well as the community in general. To this end, we constantly visit each other as well as support each other in ways that improve our quality of life.

ICT & MEDIA

We believe that technology will play a huge role in improving livelihoods in our communities. We are always looking to implement ICT projects and have in the past facilitated purchase of laptops for a remote school in Nakuru, Kenya.

TALENT & ARTS

At Nipe Sauti, we firmly believe in helping people nurture and develop their different talents. Therefore, we organize activities including football tournaments as well as singing competitions that can help members improve their talents.

About us

Nipe Sauti Africa is a charity organization striving to provide a voice to the voiceless in the society.

Our main aim is to put smiles on folks who would otherwise never be able to smile.
